# The Amphoreus Experiment
What is the prime mover of life?

I do not know, but I know it to be not The Destruction.

For Destruction is not what moves my love for life.

What moves my heart... is it rather, perhaps, the tragedy in our lives?

I do not wish to say it, but it is perhaps tragedy which moves me.

Tragedy, the "down" to life's "ups," that moves me towards tomorrow.

I wish I could say

that it is love itself that is the prime mover

of the world.

But I cannot. I know it not to be true.

...

My love...

...

See you tomorrow.

"Tomorrow"

the most beautiful prophecy of all.
