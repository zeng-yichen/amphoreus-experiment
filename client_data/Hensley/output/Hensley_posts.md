POST 8 THEME: Explaining the 'impossible math' of ultra-rare disease trials and how leveraging the FDA's Humanitarian Device Exemption (HDE) pathway allows startups to trade the front-end burden of a randomized trial for the back-end burden of perpetual surveillance.
-------------------------

It sounds like a regulatory failure. It is actually a mathematical necessity.

But for ultra-rare diseases, traditional statistics simply break.

**𝗧𝗵𝗲 𝗦𝘁𝗮𝗻𝗱𝗮𝗿𝗱 𝗜𝗻𝗱𝗶𝗰𝗮𝘁𝗶𝗼𝗻:**

Bypassing the clinical trial phase saves millions of dollars upfront.
